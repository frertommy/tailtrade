Why off-season matters

The off-season is when fundamentals get rewritten. Closing markets concentrates months of information into a single violent reopen gap. We stay live.

Transfers + Free Agency
The single biggest driver of team strength. A confirmed star signing reprices the entire team baseline — and it happens in the off-season.
Manager + Staff Changes
A sacking or hire changes win probability, playing style, and market sentiment overnight. Overwhelmingly an off-season event.
Injury Returns + Surgery
Star player availability is a first-order pricing input. Rehab timelines and preseason fitness — tradeable signals that only exist between seasons.
